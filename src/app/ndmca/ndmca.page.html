About Us North Dakota Motor Carriers Association
The North Dakota Motor Carriers Association (NDMCA) is a 501(c)6, membership based organization, first founded in 1934. As the leading education and advocacy organization for the transportation industry in North Dakota, NDMCA promotes and represents trucking interests on a state and federal level. The NDMCA provides crucial information on issues facing the industry as well as essential business services to our members. The NDMCA is led by 31 person Board of Directors, representative of the different segments of the trucking industry.
